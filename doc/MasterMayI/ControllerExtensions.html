Method Details

- (Object) protects_restful_actions

Adds before filters to your InheritedResources controller that protect access to the resource.

Only the new, create, show, edit, update, and destroy actions are covered. Note that this does not protect the index action, or attempt to limit the records that are returned by find(:all).

The before filters make use of the deny_acces helper to set the flash, save the intended location, and redirect the user.
